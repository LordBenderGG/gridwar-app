## Long Description (EN) - 4000 chars max

GRIDWAR is the most intense real-time Tic-Tac-Toe game ever made. Challenge real players, use powerful wildcards, climb the ranks, and prove you're the best.

REAL-TIME MULTIPLAYER
Challenge any online player to a best-of-3 match. 30 seconds per turn. No bots. No waiting. Pure skill.

8 GAME-CHANGING WILDCARDS
Turn the tide of battle with powerful abilities:
- Freeze: Skip your opponent's turn
- Teleport: Move your piece anywhere
- Shield: Block enemy wildcards
- Earthquake: Scramble all enemy pieces
- Sabotage: Relocate their last move
- Confusion: Invert their board view
- Turbo: Reset your timer to 30s
- Time Reduce: Cut their timer to 15s

7-TIER RANKING SYSTEM
Start as a Rookie and climb through Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, Master, and Legend. Your rank is visible to everyone.

HALL OF FAME & HALL OF SHAME
The top players earn glory in the Hall of Fame. The worst losers get publicly displayed in the Hall of Shame. Everyone can see you.

TOURNAMENTS
Create or join elimination-style tournaments with up to 16 players. Compete for gem prizes and eternal bragging rights.

DAILY MISSIONS & ACHIEVEMENTS
Complete daily challenges to earn gems. Unlock 15+ achievements as you play.

IN-GAME ECONOMY
Earn gems from victories, missions, and tournaments. Spend them in the Shop on wildcards, cosmetics, profile frames, name colors, and more.

CONSEQUENCES
Lose a match? 3-hour block from challenging. Reject a challenge? -50 points + 30-minute block. GRIDWAR doesn't forgive.

GLOBAL & LOCAL MODE
Play against anyone worldwide or only players on your WiFi network.

Download GRIDWAR now. The board is waiting. No mercy.
